Our Strategic Vision

This portal was created to simplify the complex data surrounding Sustainable Development Goal 4. We believe that by presenting information in an aesthetically pleasing and accessible way, we can encourage more students and teachers to engage with global educational issues.

Technical Stack: Developed using semantically structured HTML5 and modern CSS3 (Flexbox & Grid). All visual assets were designed to follow strictly professional academic standards.
