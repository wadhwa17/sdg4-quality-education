Strategic 2030 Targets

The roadmap to global educational equity.

TARGET 4.1

Universal Primary & Secondary

Ensure all girls and boys complete free, equitable, and quality primary and secondary education.

TARGET 4.3

Vocational & Tertiary

Equal access for all to affordable and quality technical, vocational, and tertiary education, including university.

TARGET 4.5

Gender Equality & Inclusion

Eliminate gender disparities in education and ensure equal access for the vulnerable and persons with disabilities.

TARGET 4.7

Sustainable Development

Ensure all learners acquire knowledge and skills needed to promote sustainable development and global citizenship.
